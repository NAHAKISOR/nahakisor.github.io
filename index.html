About Quest

" இன்றைய கனா! நாளைய விடை!!! "


Quest is an enthralling cultural frolic and an interschool extravaganza which unites students from pre- school to higher secondary, where they can ignite their minds, abilities, artistic sense thereby provide a path for “Change” in the society. Quest was started in the year 1997 to mark the 50th year of our independence. Quest prolongs to augment values and cognizance youth to various themes.
